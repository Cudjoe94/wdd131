Site Name

Healthy Meal Prep and Nutrition

This site provides healthy meal prep tips, recipes, and nutritional guidance.

Site Purpose

Promoting healthy eating habits through easy-to-follow meal prep strategies, recipes, and nutrition tips.

Planned Pages

  • Home Page: Introduction to healthy meal prep, benefits, site overview, call-to-action buttons.
  • Recipes Page: Categorized recipes (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks) with images, ingredients, instructions, and nutritional info.
  • Tips & Resources Page: Articles on meal prep strategies, storage tips, nutrition advice, and resource links.
  • Contact Page: A form for users to submit questions or suggestions.

Scenarios

  • What are some quick and healthy meal prep ideas for busy weekdays?
  • How can I make my meal prep more balanced and nutritious?
  • What are some effective storage methods to keep prepped meals fresh longer?
  • How can I use a meal prep checklist to stay organized?

Dynamic Features

  • Interactive recipe filtering for easy browsing.
  • Meal prep checklist stored in localStorage.
  • "Random Recipe" button to suggest a meal.

Color Schema

Main Colors:

  • #006699 - Headings, buttons
  • #f4f4f9 - Background
  • #e7eff6 - List items background

Typography

Main Font: Arial, sans-serif (used for all text)
